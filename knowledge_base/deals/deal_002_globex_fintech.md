# Deal: Globex Financial

## Overview
- **Company:** Globex Financial
- **Industry:** Fintech
- **Size:** Enterprise
- **Employee Count:** 2,500
- **Deal Value:** $180,000 ARR
- **Outcome:** Won
- **Close Date:** 2025-06-20
- **Tags:** saas, multi-year, fintech, enterprise, security

## Deal Summary
Globex Financial is a leading fintech company providing payment processing solutions. They needed a robust platform to manage their growing sales operations across multiple regions.

## Key Success Factors
1. SOC 2 Type II certification was a must-have - we had it
2. Multi-region deployment capability matched their expansion plans
3. Executive alignment with their VP of Sales early on
4. Custom pricing for 3-year commitment
5. Dedicated implementation support team

## Sales Cycle
- **Duration:** 90 days
- **Stakeholders:** VP Sales (champion), CISO (security), Procurement (contracts)
- **Meetings:** 8 calls + 2 on-site visits + security review

## Objections Overcome
- "We need SOC 2 compliance" → Provided full audit report within 24 hours
- "Your pricing is higher than competitors" → Demonstrated TCO savings over 3 years
- "We need custom SLA terms" → Legal team accommodated 99.9% uptime guarantee

## Competitive Landscape
Competed against Beta Systems and Alpha Corp. Beta was eliminated due to lack of SOC 2. Alpha lost on pricing flexibility.

## Lessons Learned
Enterprise fintech deals require patience and thorough security reviews. Having SOC 2 documentation ready is essential. Multi-year deals require executive sponsorship from both sides.
