# Deal: MedTech Inc

## Overview
- **Company:** MedTech Inc
- **Industry:** Healthcare
- **Size:** Mid-market
- **Employee Count:** 450
- **Deal Value:** $75,000 ARR
- **Outcome:** Won
- **Close Date:** 2025-08-15
- **Tags:** saas, annual, healthcare, compliance

## Deal Summary
MedTech Inc is a healthcare technology company specializing in patient management systems. They were evaluating solutions to improve their internal operations and needed a platform that could handle HIPAA compliance requirements.

## Key Success Factors
1. Emphasized HIPAA compliance features from the first meeting
2. Provided healthcare-specific case studies showing ROI
3. Offered flexible payment terms (quarterly billing option)
4. Executive sponsor (CTO) engaged early in the process
5. Demonstrated integration with their existing EMR system

## Sales Cycle
- **Duration:** 45 days
- **Stakeholders:** CTO (champion), CFO (budget), Compliance Officer (technical)
- **Meetings:** 4 calls + 1 on-site demo

## Objections Overcome
- "Your competitor has more healthcare clients" → Showed our compliance certifications and audit reports
- "Budget is tight this quarter" → Offered quarterly billing instead of annual upfront
- "Integration concerns with our EMR" → Provided technical architecture document and API documentation

## Competitive Landscape
Primary competitor was Alpha Corp. We won because of our superior compliance documentation and willingness to accommodate their billing preferences.

## Lessons Learned
Healthcare deals require compliance documentation upfront. Always lead with security and HIPAA certifications. Decision cycles involve compliance officers who need detailed technical documentation.

## Follow-up Opportunities
- Upsell to enterprise tier in 6 months (they mentioned expansion plans)
- Referral to sister company MedTech Labs
