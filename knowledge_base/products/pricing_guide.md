## Common Pricing Scenarios

### Mid-Market Healthcare (50-200 users)
- Recommend: Enterprise edition (HIPAA compliance)
- Typical deal: $50,000 - $150,000 ARR
- Discount: 10-15% for annual commitment
- Must include: BAA, compliance documentation

### Enterprise Fintech (200+ users)
- Recommend: Enterprise with custom SLA
- Typical deal: $150,000 - $500,000 ARR
- Discount: 15-20% for multi-year
- Must include: SOC 2 report, security review

### Startup (10-50 users)
- Recommend: Professional edition
- Typical deal: $10,000 - $50,000 ARR
- Discount: Minimal (5-10%)
- Watch for: Funding stage, churn risk

## Competitive Pricing

### vs Alpha Corp
They're typically 20% cheaper on list price. Counter with:
- TCO analysis (we include support, they charge extra)
- Compliance costs (they don't have HIPAA)
- Integration costs (limited API = more custom work)

### vs Beta Systems
They're 30-50% more expensive. Use this as advantage:
- Same enterprise features at lower cost
- Faster implementation saves money
- No expensive professional services required

## Payment Terms

### Standard
- Annual prepayment (preferred)
- Quarterly payment (+5% premium)
- Monthly payment (Enterprise only, +10% premium)

### Invoicing
- Net 30 for standard customers
- Net 45 for enterprise (if requested)
- Net 60 requires CFO approval

## What We Don't Discount
- Implementation services (fixed cost)
- Training packages
- Premium support tiers
- Compliance add-ons (BAA, etc.)

## Escalation Path
1. Standard discount (up to 15%): AE approval
2. Enhanced discount (15-25%): Manager approval
3. Deep discount (25-30%): Director approval
4. Exceptional (30%+): VP approval + justification
