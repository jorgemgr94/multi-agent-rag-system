# Product Overview

## What We Do
We provide a comprehensive sales operations platform that helps B2B companies manage their entire sales cycle from prospecting to close to expansion.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Deal Management
- Visual pipeline with customizable stages
- Deal scoring and prioritization
- Win/loss analysis and reporting
- Multi-currency support

### 2. Contact & Account Management
- 360-degree customer view
- Relationship mapping
- Activity tracking and logging
- Account hierarchy support

### 3. Sales Intelligence
- AI-powered deal insights
- Competitive intelligence integration
- Market and industry data
- Buyer intent signals

### 4. Workflow Automation
- Automated task creation
- Email sequences and templates
- Approval workflows
- Custom triggers and actions

### 5. Reporting & Analytics
- Real-time dashboards
- Custom report builder
- Forecasting and projections
- Territory analytics

### 6. Integrations
- Native CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Email (Gmail, Outlook)
- Calendar synchronization
- REST API + webhooks
- 100+ app integrations
